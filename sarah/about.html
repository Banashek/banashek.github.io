About Me

I like creating things. Whether it's programming, cooking, baking, or gardening, I find it so fulfilling to experience the (sometimes literal) fruits of my labor.

After a decade of working with middle school students, I decided to take a leap and try a new career. Luckily my time as a math teacher allowed me to practice patience and logical thinking, and develop my communication skills, all of which have already been useful in the projects I have built.
